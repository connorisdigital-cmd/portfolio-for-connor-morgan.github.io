About Me & Experience

Hello, I'm Connor!

I’m an experienced social media manager with a strong background in digital content creation and information technology, combined with a genuine passion for dogs and previous experience as a former trainer. I specialise in building engaging, authentic online prescenses for pet-focused brands through high-quality photography, designs that tell a story and well-optimised content but I have a wide range of skills allowing me to be flexible in each industry I attend to.

My background allows me to create content that not only looks great but resonates with the viewer, from capturing natural visuals to designing cohesive branding and managing SEO growth, I bring both creativity and strategy to every project.

Some samples includes concept and recreated examples due to NDA restrictions and client projects. These pieces are intended to demonstrate my approach to content creation, editing and brand understanding.
